Tools

Filters

Filters methods are meant to filter through a list of locators resuling from a find method or native playwright method.

Inspect

Inspect methods allow easy search of different kind of elements in a page.

Interact

Interact methods offers a multitude of ways to interact with the DOM without the constraint of boilerplate code.

Navigate

Navigates methods offers a multitude of ways to go to other pages, but also load more content inside a page by scrolling an element.

Wait

Timing is a huge pain to handle when using a browser’s driver. This Wait module tries to alliviate this pain with more precise methods.
